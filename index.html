Key Features and Benefits

The Bilstein 5125 Series Shocks are loaded with features designed to outperform stock and many aftermarket alternatives. Here’s why this upgrade makes sense for your Toyota:

1. Long Travel Stroke Options

Available in 10-inch, 12-inch, and 14-inch stroke lengths, the Bilstein 5125 Series Shocks provide the extended travel range essential for custom lift setups, ensuring your suspension performs as hard as you do on the trail. The 10-inch stroke option features an extended length of 25.93 inches and a collapsed length of 15.91 inches. The 12-inch stroke extends to 29.70 inches and collapses to 17.91 inches, while the 14-inch stroke reaches a full extension of 34.90 inches and compresses down to 20.75 inches. These travel options allow you to fine-tune your suspension for maximum articulation, stability, and ride comfort on any terrain.

2. 255/70 Valving

This precise valving configuration is tuned to provide a perfect balance between comfort on the highway and control in demanding off-road conditions. It ensures smooth compression and firm rebound when you need it most.

3. Zinc-Plated Finish

Off-road conditions are tough on suspension components. The zinc plating adds an extra layer of corrosion resistance, protecting your investment from mud, water, and road salt.

4. Monotube Gas Pressure Technology

This design maintains consistent performance by eliminating shock fade, keeping your ride predictable and smooth even after miles of punishing terrain.

5. Universal Fitment

These shocks are not application-specific, making them ideal for custom suspension setups. However, proper measurement and fitment are critical. If you’re unsure, refer to the provided stroke length chart or consult with our team.

Important Fitment Notes

If you’re fitting these shocks to the rear of a 1984-2004 Toyota Pickup or a leaf-spring-equipped 4Runner with stock mounts, the bushings will need to be replaced with specific shock bushings for proper fitment. It’s a simple modification, but an important one to ensure longevity and correct installation.

These shocks are versatile but require accurate measurements to match them to your build. Use the travel length chart as a guide, or reach out to us for fitment assistance.
